Common Driving Faults — Notes

Examples (not exhaustive): blind spot checks missing or late; moving off unsafely; ineffective observations before manoeuvres; stalling or repeated stalling; poor co‑ordination of controls; incorrect signalling; approaching junctions too fast/slow; cutting corners; unsafe overtaking; not reacting to traffic lights/controllers; holding up following traffic; undue hesitation; inappropriate lane discipline; stopping on crossings; failure to comply with mandatory signs, and more (based on DL25 notes).
